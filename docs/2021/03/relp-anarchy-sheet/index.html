Relationship Anarchy Worksheet

2021 Mar 21, Timid Robot Zehta

The RelpAnarchySheet.pdf (152 KB) is a worksheet designed with the goal of making relationship negotiatings more successful. I really liked the various Relationship Anarchy Smorgasbords, but I wanted a more interactive tool.
